I am a Senior Data Manager and trained Geomorphologist. I managed and/or contributed with many international and multidisciplinary teams and projects, ranging from scientific data management solutions to web-based geospatial information systems and from renewable energy assessment, to infrastructure development, climate change, permafrost, small communities well-being and ecological integrity. I love hiking the mountains of Iceland and go camping with my 5 years old boy for sharing with him this beautiful world and its nature. I am polyvalent and determined in what I do.
